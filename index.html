Our Story

Stephanie and Corey first met in 2013 on OkCupid. She loved to write, and it was the short stories she posted on her dating profile that caught Corey's attention. He continued to visit the profile as much for the creepy stories that changed often as for the striking redhead authoring them, and after a while Stephanie took notice. Within days of meeting over coffee they were both crazy about each other and making plans to meet parents. Stephanie loved Corey's passion for family, art, and discovery. Corey admired Stephanie's strength, creativity, and intelligence.

They moved in together, blended families, and made a baby—all while Corey was making plans to marry her. He proposed a week before Christmas in 2015. They recently purchased a home in Avondale that they share with two feral children called Evelyn and Atticus, Nickel The Dog, a panther named Diego, a group of monarch butterflies, and about a million succulents.
