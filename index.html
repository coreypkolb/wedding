Our Story

Stephanie and Corey first met in the fall of 2013 over OkCupid. She loved to write, and it was the short stories she posted on her dating profile that caught Corey's attention. He continued to visit the profile as much for the creepy stories that changed often as for the striking redhead authoring them, and after a while Stephanie took notice.

Within days of meeting over coffee they were both crazy about each other. Stephanie loved Corey's passion for family, art, and discovery. Corey admired Stephanie's strength, creativity, and intelligence. They quickly moved in together. Families were blended, babies were made, and Corey started making plans to marry her. He asked her at first unofficially, and then a week before Christmas in 2015, he did again in San Marco Square—with ring and all.

They recently purchased a home in Avondale. They share their home with two feral children, Evelyn and Atticus, a dog Nickel, a panther named Diego, a group of monarch butterflies, and about a million succulents.
